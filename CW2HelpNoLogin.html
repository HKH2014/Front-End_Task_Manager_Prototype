Why can't I see my tabs?

Consider Refreshing the webpage. Alternatively, you can swap to a different browser.

How can I join a team?

Simply click on the 'team collaboration' button on the sidebar. To join an existing team, press the 'join team' button. To create a new team, click the 'add team' and fill in the required details.
